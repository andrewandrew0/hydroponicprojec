The Benefits of Hydroponics

Hydroponics is a sustainable agricultural method that unlike traditional methods, uses water instead of soil to grow crops. The plants get their nutrients from a special nutrient-rich mixture. Hydroponics has many benefits over traditional farming:

  • Uses 90% less water
  • Harsher environment for weeds
  • Has 25%-25% higher crop yields than other methods
  • Grows 40%-50% faster than soil-based plants
  • Healthier and stronger plants
  • Less maintenance is needed (less daily watering, and nutrient supplement)
  • No need to change the water system
  • Not as susceptible to pests and diseases
  • It is an automatable process
  • Water is reusable which is super duper recyclable
  • Can use up to 4x less space than traditional cultivation methods

    • Now, you may ask: What are the essentials to starting your own hydroponic farm?

    Essential Nutrients and Materials

    In order to build a good hydroponic farm, you will need water mixed with essential nutrients to plant growth. This is the only maintenance the farm will require (this can be automated!). The nutrients can be divided into 2 groups: Macronutrients and Micronutrients.

    Macronutrients

    Macronutrients consists of the main 3 nutrients. Hydroponics requires these nutrients the most

  • Nitrogen (in the form of nitrate)
  • Phosphorus (in the form of phosphate)
  • Potassium (in the form of potassium sulfate)

    • Micronutrients

    Micronutrients are the other essential nutrients. They are essential, but you don't need as much quantity
